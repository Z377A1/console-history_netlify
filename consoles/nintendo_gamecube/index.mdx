The Nintendo GameCube is a home video game console developed by Nintendo. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GameCube))

Details:
- Name for Console: Nintendo GameCube
- Alias: GameCube
- Year Released: 2001
- Discontinued: 2007
- Developer: Nintendo
- Product Family: Nintendo
- Generation Number: 6th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
	- [Game Boy Player](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Game_Boy_Player)
- Specifications:
	- CPU: IBM Gekko
	- GPU: ATI Hollywood
	- Memory: 40 MB RAM
	- Storage: MiniDVD discs
	- Display Formats: 480i, 480p
	- Sound: Stereo
	- Controller Input: Nintendo GameCube Controller
	- Extra: Handle for portability, Broadband Adapter
- Units Sold: 21.74 million units (As of [September 2011](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GameCube))
- Predecessor: [Nintendo 64](../nintendo-64)
- Successor: [Wii](../wii)
- Company Website: [Nintendo GameCube](https://www.nintendo.co.uk/Hardware/Nintendo-History/Nintendo-GameCube/Nintendo-GameCube-627129.html)
