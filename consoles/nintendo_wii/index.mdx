The Nintendo Wii is a home video game console developed by Nintendo. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wii))

Details:
- Name for Console: Nintendo Wii
- Alias: Wii
- Year Released: 2006
- Discontinued: October 20, 2013
- Developer: Nintendo
- Product Family: Nintendo
- Generation Number: 7th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
	- [Nintendo GameCube](../nintendo-gamecube)
- Specifications:
	- CPU: IBM PowerPC "Broadway"
	- GPU: ATI Hollywood
	- Memory: 88 MB RAM
	- Storage: 512 MB flash memory, SD card support
	- Display Formats: 480i, 480p
	- Sound: Stereo
	- Controller Input: Wii Remote, Nunchuk, Classic Controller
	- Extra: Motion controls, Virtual Console
- Units Sold: 101.6 million units (As of [September 2023](https://www.statista.com/statistics/1101890/unit-sales-nintendo-wii-region/#:~:text=First%20released%20in%20November%202006,33%20million%20units%20in%20Europe.))
- Predecessor: [Nintendo GameCube](../nintendo-gamecube)
- Successor: [Wii U](../nintendo-wii-u)
- Company Website: [Nintendo Wii](https://www.nintendo.co.za/Wii/Wii-94559.html)
