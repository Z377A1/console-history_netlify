The Sega Saturn is a home video game console developed by Sega. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sega_Saturn))
Details:
- Name for Console: Sega Saturn
- Alias: Saturn
- Year Released: 1994
- Year Stopped Manufacturing: 2000
- Developer: Sega
- Product Family: Sega
- Generation Number: 5th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Limited
- Specifications:
- CPU: Hitachi SH-2, Dual CPUs
- GPU: VDP1, VDP2
- Memory: 2 MB RAM
- Storage: CD-ROM drive
- Display Formats: 240p, 480i
- Sound: Stereo
- Controller Input: Sega Saturn Controller
- Extra: CD-based games, 2D and 3D graphics
- Units Sold: Approximately 9.5 million units (As of [September 1999](https://www.gamespot.com/articles/sega-saturn-sold-9-5-million/1100-2460006/))
- Predecessor: [Sega Genesis](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Sega_Genesis)
- Successor: [Sega Dreamcast](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Sega_Dreamcast)
- Company Website: N/A (Sega's official website is for modern products)
