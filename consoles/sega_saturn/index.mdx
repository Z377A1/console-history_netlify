The Sega Saturn is a home video game console developed by Sega. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sega_Saturn))

Details:
- Name for Console: Sega Saturn
- Alias: Saturn
- Year Released: 1994
- Discontinued: 2000
- Developer: Sega
- Product Family: Sega
- Generation Number: 5th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Limited
- Specifications:
	- CPU: Hitachi SH-2, Dual CPUs
	- GPU: VDP1, VDP2
	- Memory: 2 MB RAM
	- Storage: CD-ROM drive
	- Display Formats: 240p, 480i
	- Sound: Stereo
	- Controller Input: Sega Saturn Controller
	- Extra: CD-based games, 2D and 3D graphics
- Units Sold: 9.26 million units (As of [November 2023](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sega_Saturn))
- Predecessor: [Sega Genesis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sega_Genesis)
- Successor: [Sega Dreamcast](../sega-dreamcast)
- Company Website: [Sega Saturn](https://segaretro.org/Sega_Saturn)
