The Nintendo Wii U is a home video game console developed by Nintendo. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Wii_U))
Details:
- Name for Console: Nintendo Wii U
- Alias: Wii U
- Year Released: 2012
- Year Stopped Manufacturing: January 31, 2017
- Developer: Nintendo
- Product Family: Nintendo
- Generation Number: 8th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
- [Wii](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Wii_U#Backward_compatibility)
- Specifications:
- CPU: IBM PowerPC "Espresso"
- GPU: AMD Radeon "Latte"
- Memory: 2 GB RAM
- Storage: 8 GB, 32 GB flash memory, expandable via USB HDD
- Display Formats: 480i, 480p, 720p, 1080i, 1080p
- Sound: Stereo
- Controller Input: Wii U GamePad, Wii U Pro Controller
- Extra: Dual-screen gameplay, Miiverse
- Units Sold: Approximately 13.56 million units (As of [September 2021](https://www.nintendo.co.jp/ir/en/finance/hard_soft/numbers/index.html))
- Predecessor: [Nintendo Wii](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Nintendo_Wii)
- Successor: [Nintendo Switch](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Nintendo_Switch)
- Company Website: [Nintendo Wii U](https://www.nintendo.com/wiiu/)
