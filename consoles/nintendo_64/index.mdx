The Nintendo 64 is a home video game console developed by Nintendo. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_64))

Details:
- Name for Console: Nintendo 64
- Alias: N64
- Year Released: 1996
- Discontinued: 2003
- Developer: Nintendo
- Product Family: Nintendo
- Generation Number: 5th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: No
- Specifications:
	- CPU: NEC VR4300
	- GPU: SGI RCP
	- Memory: 4 MB RDRAM
	- Storage: Cartridges
	- Display Formats: 240p, 480i
	- Sound: Stereo
	- Controller Input: Nintendo 64 Controller
	- Extra: First console with 3D analog stick
- Units Sold: Approximately 32.9 million units (As of [November 2015](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_64))
- Predecessor: [Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Nintendo_Entertainment_System)
- Successor: [Nintendo GameCube](../nintendo-gamecube)
- Company Website: [Nintendo 64](https://www.nintendo.co.uk/Hardware/Nintendo-History/Nintendo-64/Nintendo-64-625959.html)
