The Nintendo GameCube is a home video game console developed by Nintendo. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_GameCube))
Details:
- Name for Console: Nintendo GameCube
- Alias: GameCube
- Year Released: 2001
- Year Stopped Manufacturing: 2007
- Developer: Nintendo
- Product Family: Nintendo
- Generation Number: 6th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
- [Game Boy Player](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Game_Boy_Player)
- Specifications:
- CPU: IBM Gekko
- GPU: ATI Hollywood
- Memory: 40 MB RAM
- Storage: MiniDVD discs
- Display Formats: 480i, 480p
- Sound: Stereo
- Controller Input: Nintendo GameCube Controller
- Extra: Handle for portability, Broadband Adapter
- Units Sold: Approximately 21.74 million units (As of [September 2021](https://www.nintendo.co.jp/ir/en/finance/hard_soft/numbers/index.html))
- Predecessor: [Nintendo 64](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Nintendo_64)
- Successor: [Wii](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Wii)
- Company Website: N/A (Nintendo's official website is for modern products)
