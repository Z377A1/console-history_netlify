The Nintendo Wii U is a home video game console developed by Nintendo. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wii_U))

Details:
- Name for Console: Nintendo Wii U
- Alias: Wii U
- Year Released: 2012
- Discontinued: January 31, 2017
- Developer: Nintendo
- Product Family: Nintendo
- Generation Number: 8th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
	- [Wii](../nintendo-wii)
- Specifications:
	- CPU: IBM PowerPC "Espresso"
	- GPU: AMD Radeon "Latte"
	- Memory: 2 GB RAM
	- Storage: 8 GB, 32 GB flash memory, expandable via USB HDD
	- Display Formats: 480i, 480p, 720p, 1080i, 1080p
	- Sound: Stereo
	- Controller Input: Wii U GamePad, Wii U Pro Controller
	- Extra: Dual-screen gameplay, Miiverse
- Units Sold: 13.56 million units (As of [December, 2019](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wii_U))
- Predecessor: [Nintendo Wii](../nintendo-wii)
- Successor: [Nintendo Switch](../nintendo-switch)
- Company Website: [Nintendo Wii U](https://www.nintendo.co.za/Wii-U/Wii-U-344102.html)
