The PlayStation 2 (PS2) is a home video game console developed by Sony Computer Entertainment. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PlayStation_2))

Details:
- Name for Console: PlayStation 2
- Alias: PS2
- Year Released: 2000
- Discontinued: January 4, 2013
- Developer: Sony Computer Entertainment
- Product Family: PlayStation
- Generation Number: 6th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
	- [PlayStation Original](../playstation-original)
- Specifications:
	- CPU: Emotion Engine
	- GPU: Graphics Synthesizer
	- Memory: 32 MB RDRAM
	- Storage: 8 MB memory card, Optional HDD
	- Display Formats: 480i, 480p, 576i, 576p, 720p, 1080i
	- Sound: Dolby Digital, DTS, 48 kHz 16-bit PCM
	- Controller Input: DualShock 2 Controller
	- Extra: DVD Player. Sony also released a Slim Refresh version, the Slim PlayStation 2 (Slimline) in the year 2004.
- Units Sold: 158.7 million units (As of [2023](https://www.kompas.id/baca/english/2023/07/18/en-penjualan-playstation-2-masih-tertinggi-sepanjang-masa))
- Predecessor: [PlayStation Original](../playstation-original)
- Successor: [PlayStation 3](../playstation-3)
- Company Website: N/A
