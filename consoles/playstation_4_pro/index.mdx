The PlayStation 4 Pro (PS4 Pro) is an enhanced version of the PlayStation 4, developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PlayStation_4_Pro))

Details:
- Name for Console: PlayStation 4 Pro
- Alias: PS4 Pro
- Year Released: 2016
- Discontinued: 2021 [*](https://www.pocket-lint.com/games/news/playstation/155153-sony-reportedly-discontinues-multiple-ps4-and-ps4-pro-models/)
- Developer: Sony Interactive Entertainment
- Product Family: PlayStation
- Generation Number: 8th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
	- [PlayStation 4](../playstation-4)
- Specifications:
	- Operating System: Customized Orbis OS
	- CPU: Custom AMD Jaguar
	- GPU: Custom AMD Polaris
	- Memory: 8 GB GDDR5
	- Storage: 1 TB HDD
	- Display Formats: 1080p, 1440p, 4K
	- Sound: Dolby and DTS 5.1 surround sound
	- Controller Input: DualShock 4 Controller
	- Extra: Enhanced Graphics and Performance
- Units Sold: 117+ Million Units (Includes the [base PlayStation 4](../playstation-4) (As of [2023](https://www.statista.com/statistics/651576/global-ps4-console-unit-sales/#:~:text=As%20of%20September%202023%2C%20cumulative,than%20in%20the%20first%20two.))
- Predecessor: [PlayStation 4](../playstation-4)
- Successor: [PlayStation 5](../playstation-5)
- Company Website: [PlayStation 4 Pro](https://www.playstation.com/ps4/ps4-pro/)
