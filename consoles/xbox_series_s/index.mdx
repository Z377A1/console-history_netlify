The Xbox Series S is a home video game console developed by Microsoft. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Xbox_Series_S))

Details:
- Name for Console: Xbox Series S
- Alias: XSS
- Year Released: 2020
- Discontinued: Ongoing
- Developer: Microsoft
- Product Family: Xbox
- Generation Number: 9th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes (With digital titles only as consoles is only availabe as digital version)
	- [Xbox One](../xbox-one)
    - [Xbox 360](../xbox-360)
    - [Original Xbox](../xbox-original)
- Specifications:
	- Operating System: Xbox OS
	- CPU: Custom 3.6 GHz 8-core AMD Zen 2
	- GPU: Custom 1.565 GHz 20 CU RDNA 2
	- Memory: 10 GB GDDR6
	- Storage: 512 GB NVMe SSD
	- Display Formats: 720p, 1080p, 1440p, 4K
	- Sound: Dolby Atmos, DTS:X
	- Controller Input: Xbox Wireless Controller
	- Extra: Digital-Only in 2 versions: White 512 GB and Black 1 TB
- Units Sold: 21+ Million Units (Including [Xbox Series X](../xbox-series-x)) (As of [2023](https://gamingbolt.com/xbox-series-x-s-has-sold-21-million-units-xbox-one-at-58-million-as-per-microsoft-brazil-presentation)).
- Predecessor: [Xbox One](../xbox-one)
- Released Along: [Xbox Series X](../xbox-series-x)
- Successor: [Leaked Xbox Series X Refresh - Brooklin](https://www.theverge.com/2023/9/19/23880111/microsoft-xbox-series-x-new-design-refresh)
