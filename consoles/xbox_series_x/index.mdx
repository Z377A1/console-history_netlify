The Xbox Series X (XSX) is the fourth generation console in the Xbox series, developed by Microsoft. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xbox_Series_X_and_Series_S))

Details:
- Name for Console: Xbox Series X
- Alias: XSX
- Year Released: 2020
- Year Stopped Manufacturing: Ongoing
- Developer: Microsoft
- Product Family: Xbox
- Generation Number: 9th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
    - [Xbox One]()
    - [Xbox 360]()
    - [Original Xbox]()
- Specifications:
    - Operating System: Xbox System Software
    - CPU: Custom AMD Zen 2
    - GPU: Custom AMD RDNA 2
    - Memory: 16 GB GDDR6
    - Storage: 1 TB NVMe SSD
    - Display Formats: 1080p, 1440p, 4K, 8K
    - Sound: Dolby Atmos
    - Controller Input: Xbox Wireless Controller (Model 1914)
- Units Sold: 21+ Million Units (Including [Xbox Series S]()) (As of [2023](https://gamingbolt.com/xbox-series-x-s-has-sold-21-million-units-xbox-one-at-58-million-as-per-microsoft-brazil-presentation).)
