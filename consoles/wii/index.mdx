The Nintendo Wii is a home video game console developed by Nintendo. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Wii))
Details:
- Name for Console: Nintendo Wii
- Alias: Wii
- Year Released: 2006
- Discontinued: October 20, 2013
- Developer: Nintendo
- Product Family: Nintendo
- Generation Number: 7th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
- [Nintendo GameCube](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Wii#Backward_compatibility)
- Specifications:
- CPU: IBM PowerPC "Broadway"
- GPU: ATI Hollywood
- Memory: 88 MB RAM
- Storage: 512 MB flash memory, SD card support
- Display Formats: 480i, 480p
- Sound: Stereo
- Controller Input: Wii Remote, Nunchuk, Classic Controller
- Extra: Motion controls, Virtual Console
- Units Sold: Over 101 million units (As of [September 2021](https://www.nintendo.co.jp/ir/en/finance/hard_soft/numbers/index.html))
- Predecessor: [Nintendo GameCube](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Nintendo_GameCube)
- Successor: [Wii U](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Wii_U)
- Company Website: [Nintendo Wii](https://www.nintendo.com/wii/)
