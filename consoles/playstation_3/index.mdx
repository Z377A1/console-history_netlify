The PlayStation 3 (PS3) is a home video game console developed by Sony Computer Entertainment. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PlayStation_3))

Details:
- Name for Console: PlayStation 3
- Alias: PS3
- Year Released: 2006
- Discontinued: October 2016
- Developer: Sony Computer Entertainment
- Product Family: PlayStation
- Generation Number: 7th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
	- [PlayStation 2](../playstation-2)
	- [PlayStation Original](../playstation-original)
- Specifications:
	- Operating System: XrossMediaBar (XMB)
	- CPU: Cell Broadband Engine
	- GPU: RSX 'Reality Synthesizer'
	- Memory: 256 MB XDR DRAM, 256 MB GDDR3
	- Storage: 20 GB, 40 GB, 60 GB, 80 GB, 160 GB, 250 GB, 320 GB HDD
	- Display Formats: 480i, 480p, 720p, 1080i, 1080p
	- Sound: LPCM 7.1ch, Dolby Digital, DTS, Dolby TrueHD, DTS-HD Master Audio
	- Controller Input: DualShock 3 Controller
	- Extra: Blu-ray Disc, Wi-Fi, HDMI. 2 refresh models were released, the Slim and Super Slim versions of the PS3.
- Units Sold: 87.4 million (As of [2023](https://www.statista.com/statistics/1101879/unit-sales-playstation-3-region/#:~:text=Sony's%20third%2Dgeneration%20console%2C%20the,30%20million%20units%20in%20Europe.))
- Predecessor: [PlayStation 2](../playstation-2)
- Slim Refresh: [PlayStation 3 Slim](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PlayStation_3_models#:~:text=produce.%5B24%5D-,Slim%20model,-%5Bedit%5D) and [PlayStation 3 Super Slim](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PlayStation_3_models#:~:text=41%5D%5B42%5D-,Super%20Slim%20model,-%5Bedit%5D)
- Successor: [PlayStation 4](../playstation-4)
- Company Website: [PlayStation 3](https://www.playstation.com/support/hardware/ps3/)
