The Nintendo Switch is a home video game console developed by Nintendo. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_Switch))
Details:
- Name for Console: Nintendo Switch
- Alias: Switch
- Year Released: 2017
- Year Stopped Manufacturing: Ongoing
- Developer: Nintendo
- Product Family: Nintendo
- Generation Number: 8th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: No
- Specifications:
- CPU: Custom NVIDIA Tegra X1
- GPU: Custom NVIDIA Tegra X1
- Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4
- Storage: 32 GB flash memory, expandable via microSD card
- Display Formats: 720p (Handheld), 1080p (TV Mode)
- Sound: Stereo
- Controller Input: Joy-Con controllers, Pro Controller
- Extra: Hybrid design (handheld and home console modes), Dock
- Units Sold: Over 89 million units (As of [September 2021](https://www.nintendo.co.jp/ir/en/finance/hard_soft/numbers/index.html))
- Predecessor: [Nintendo Wii U](https://en.wikipedia.org.org/wiki/Nintendo_Wii_U)
- Successor: N/A (As of my knowledge cutoff date in September 2021)
