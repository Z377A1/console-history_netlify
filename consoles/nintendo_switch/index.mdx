The Nintendo Switch is a home video game console developed by Nintendo. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_Switch))

Details:
- Name for Console: Nintendo Switch
- Alias: Switch
- Year Released: 2017
- Discontinued: Ongoing
- Developer: Nintendo
- Product Family: Nintendo
- Generation Number: 8th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: No
- Specifications:
	- CPU: Custom NVIDIA Tegra X1
	- GPU: GM20B Graphics Processor
	- Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4
	- Storage: 32 GB flash memory, expandable via microSD card
	- Display Formats: 720p (Handheld), 1080p (TV Mode)
	- Sound: Stereo
	- Controller Input: Joy-Con controllers, Pro Controller
	- Extra: Hybrid design (handheld and home console modes), Dock. 2 models have been released since then by Nintendo, which are the [Switch Oled](https://www.nintendo.com/us/switch/oled-model/) and the [Switch Lite](https://www.nintendo.com/us/switch/lite/).
- Units Sold: 129.5+ million units (As of [20 October, 2023](https://www.statista.com/statistics/687059/nintendo-switch-unit-sales-worldwide/))
- Predecessor: [Nintendo Wii U](../nintendo-wii-u)
- Successor: N/A
- Company Website: [Nintendo Switch](https://www.nintendo.com/switch/)
