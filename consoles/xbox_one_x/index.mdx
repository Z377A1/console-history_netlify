The Xbox One X is a home video game console developed by Microsoft. ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xbox_One))

Details:
- Name for Console: Xbox One X
- Alias: None
- Year Released: 2017
- Discontinued: July 2020
- Developer: Microsoft
- Product Family: Xbox
- Generation Number: 8th Generation
- Backward Compatibility: Yes
	- [Xbox One](../xbox-one)
	- [Xbox 360](../xbox-360)
	- [Xbox Original](../xbox-original)
- Specifications:
	- Operating System: Xbox OS
	- CPU: Custom 2.3 GHz 8-core AMD Jaguar
	- GPU: Custom 1172 MHz 40 CU AMD Polaris
	- Memory: 12 GB GDDR5
	- Storage: 1 TB HDD
	- Display Formats: 1080p, 1440p, 4K
	- Sound: DTS 5.1, Dolby Digital 5.1, TrueHD with Atmos
	- Controller Input: Xbox Wireless Controller (Model 1708)
	- Extra: 4K UHD Blu-ray drive
- Units Sold: Nearly 58 million units (Includes [Xbox One versions](../xbox-one)) (As of [7 September, 2023](https://www.statista.com/statistics/1005418/xbox-one-console-unit-sales-country/))
- Predecessor: [Xbox One](../xbox-one)
- Successor: [Xbox Series X](../xbox-series-x) and [Xbox Series S](../xbox-series-s)
- Company Website: [Xbox One X](https://www.xbox.com/consoles/xbox-one-x)
